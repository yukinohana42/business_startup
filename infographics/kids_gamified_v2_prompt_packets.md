# Kids Gamified v2 Infographic Prompt Packets

## Common Direction

Use these prompts for a more exciting v2 visual layer.
v1 materials remain unchanged.

Visual style:

- 16:9 landscape educational infographic.
- Original high-energy Japanese youth adventure illustration.
- Clean line art, cel-shaded color, dynamic camera angle, readable composition.
- Middle-school friendly, not childish.
- Warm but exciting.
- Characters are original only.
- Use quest boards, cards, notebooks, case files, radars, small lab tools, and mini stages.
- Japanese text should be added later in layout. Do not rely on generated text.

Negative prompt for every image:

```text
No existing anime, manga, game, movie, logo, brand, mascot, or character. No imitation of a specific artist or franchise style. No recognizable character silhouette, outfit, hair, symbol, creature, logo, or power effect from an existing work. No ranking, score, trophy, battle victory, money worship, fear-based career imagery, or mocking of people who are struggling. No dense text.
```

Save candidate images under:

```text
assets/generated/gamified_v2/dayX/page_XX/candidates/
```

Do not place images in `final/` before human review.

## Day1: Value Quest Opening / 6 pages

### Page 1: 価値クエスト開始

Purpose: Day1のミッション導入。

Visual message: 仕事とお金の正体を探る冒険が始まる。

Prompt:

```text
Create a 16:9 high-energy educational adventure infographic. Scene: an original middle-school boy detective, Yuu, stands at the entrance of a bright "Value Quest" town map with a notebook, magnifying glass, and three glowing clue cards: work, money, value represented by simple icons only. A friendly navigator character points to a mission board. Dynamic but clean composition, cel-shaded original youth adventure illustration, navy and coral accents, warm cream sky, no readable text required, large empty space for later Japanese title.
```

Later text:

```text
Day1 価値クエスト開始
仕事とお金の正体を見破れ
```

Review checklist:

- ユウが観察を始めているか。
- 仕事、お金、価値が手がかりとして見えるか。
- 既存作品に見えないか。

### Page 2: モヤモヤを変える

Purpose: 価値をBefore/Afterで理解する。

Visual message: 価値とは、相手の状態が少し良くなること。

Prompt:

```text
Create a 16:9 adventure infographic showing a before/after transformation. Left side: a student is stuck before a test, surrounded by small original "moyamoya" cloud creatures representing confusion and worry. Right side: the same student has a simple first-step checklist and looks relieved, the moyamoya creatures turning into small glowing helper seeds. Yuu connects the two sides with a value-change card arrow. Clean action, no mocking, warm high-energy original illustration.
```

Later text:

```text
価値 = 相手の変化
わからない -> できそう
```

Review checklist:

- Beforeの人を笑いものにしていないか。
- Afterが大げさすぎないか。
- 変化がひと目でわかるか。


- ありがとうの流れに見えるか。
- 勝敗や稼ぐ偉さに見えないか。
